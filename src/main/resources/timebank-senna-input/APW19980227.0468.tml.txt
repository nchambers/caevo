MOSCOW ( AP ) _ The mayor of Moscow has allocated funds to help build a museum in honor of Mikhail Kalashnikov , the Russian who gave his name to the world 's most widely wielded weapon , according to a news agency report Friday .
Kalashnikov designed the AK-47 automatic rifle , famed for its reliability and effectiveness .
Together with its derivatives , the Kalashnikov rifle has been used by the police and armies of 55 countries and an assortment of guerrillas , terrorists and just plain thugs around the globe .
Seventy-five million copies of the rifle have been built since it entered production in February 1947 , making it history 's most widely distributed weapon .
